Automobile EDA — Pricing & Performance

Kaggle Dataset · 205 Automobiles · 26 Features

Problem Statement

Understand how vehicle specifications influence pricing, performance, and fuel efficiency. The analysis aims to identify the key factors that drive automobile prices and uncover patterns in the dataset.

Approach

  • Data Inspection: Reviewed shape, data types, and summary statistics; identified missing values.
  • Data Cleaning: Handled missing values via mean/median/mode imputation; standardised features.
  • Univariate Analysis: Analysed distributions of price, engine size, horsepower; identified skewness and outliers.
  • Bivariate & Multivariate: Correlation heatmap to identify strong predictors of price.
205Records
26Features
4Key Insights

Key Insights

  • Engine size, horsepower, and curb weight show strong positive correlation with price.
  • Mileage has a negative relationship with price.
  • Fuel type and body style significantly impact price distribution.
  • Higher performance vehicles are generally priced higher across all segments.
